Mechanical Winch Project

Principles of Engineering

October 16, 2017 - October 20, 2017

The objective of this project was to design, create, and test a small scale prototype of a mechanical winch able to lift an object of 100 grams up 30 centimeters, as well as create a NetLogo simulation of it. For this project, I was the primary NetLogo programmer. I furthered my knowledge of NetLogo code, specifically programming movement of turtles in NetLogo. I came to a problem when I pressed 'go' on the interface, the weight would spin, as well as the drum. I eventually solved this problem by asking only the Drum turtle to spin when the go function was called, and also by restricting the weight's movement to a single x-coordinate.
