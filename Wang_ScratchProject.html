SCRATCH PROJECT

Computer Science Principles

August 30, 2018 - September 7, 2018

↩ Go back


Instructions: Use the arrow keys to move. Avoid the green circles and reach the blue area to proceed to the next level. On level 2, press the space bar while touching the grey blocks in order to 'scrap' (remove) them.


The objective of this project was to design and create a game using MIT's Scratch visual programming tool. During the project, I fostered a collaborative and productive environment by participating in pair programming with my partner. Through this project I deepened my understanding of fundamental coding principles such as loops and "if, then" statements. One challenge during this project was implementing a feature in which blocks could be removed from the level, then replaced. This taught us the importance of prioritizing certain objectives and managing time. Ultimately our team decided to remove the feature of replacing the blocks due to time constraints. This choice taught me how to make difficult decisions under pressure (in this case, time pressure).
